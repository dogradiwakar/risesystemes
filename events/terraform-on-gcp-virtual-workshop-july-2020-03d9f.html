How to Eliminate the Complexity and Cost of Kubernetes Storage with Redapt and Hammerspace

Written by Redapt Marketing | Jul 6, 2020 7:53:04 PM

Enterprises everywhere are going through a digital transformation, adopting new technologies like Kubernetes to improve the ability to rapidly react to business demands.

As one of the most popular container orchestration platforms, Kubernetes simplifies the development, deployment, and scaling of containerized workloads. Kubernetes was built to be infrastructure agnostic, but as soon as data becomes persistent, the challenges are often viewed as storage infrastructure problems.

Specialized storage solutions for Kubernetes are not the answer; they just create new data silos and compromise data agility by forcing developers to navigate storage infrastructure. Hammerspace helps automate data management for Kubernetes across clouds and clusters.
