Redapt Webinar: Ensuring Governance & Security in the Age of Remote Working with Windows Virtual Desktop

February 10, 2021 | 10:00 am PST

Sign up for our free presentation on how your enterprise can utilize WVD and the cloud to keep your data and applications secure.

Register for Webinar

Webinar Details

  • Event: Ensuring Governance & Security in the Age of Remote Working with Windows Virtual Desktop
  • Date: Wednesday, January 13
  • Time: 10:00 am PST
  • Location: Zoom (link will be included in an email once registered)

Visit Event Website

Keep your data and applications secure even with your teams working remotely.

Join Redapt expert Ryan Otis for a free 45-minute discussion on how your organization can fully leverage Windows Virtual Desktop (WVD) and the cloud to ensure your data continues to have proper governance and security—even when your teams are spread far and wide.

In this conversation, you will learn:

  • Current challenges enterprises face with governance and security in this age of remote work
  • How WVD and the cloud are changing the way enterprises think of data access
  • The benefits of WVD environments beyond governance and security

Reserve your spot today!
