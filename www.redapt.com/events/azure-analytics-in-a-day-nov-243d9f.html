Analytics in a Day Virtual Workshop

Written by Redapt Marketing | Nov 4, 2020 7:27:28 PM

As a Microsoft Gold Partner, Redapt is pleased to invite you to this upcoming Analytics in a Day virtual workshop, and we encourage you to explore our Azure capabilities and reach out with any questions. 

This half-day, virtual workshop and hands-on lab incorporates all the latest features of Azure Synapse Analytics that are currently in public preview. See how you can accelerate time to insight with the unified experience that brings data ingestion, enterprise data warehousing, and big data analytics into a single service.

In our workshop, data professionals will better understand how to:

  • Streamline cloud analytics pipelines
  • Build a scalable modern data warehouse
  • Ensure flexibility with business needs

The last 90 minutes of the workshop will be dedicated to hands-on training to help get you started on your cloud analytics journey. The lab will focus on building an end-to-end analytics solution with Azure Synapse and Power BI.
