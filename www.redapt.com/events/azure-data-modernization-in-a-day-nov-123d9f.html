Data Modernization in a Day Virtual Workshop

Written by Redapt Marketing | Nov 4, 2020 3:00:27 AM

As a Microsoft Gold Partner, Redapt is pleased to invite you to this upcoming Data Modernization in a Day Workshop, and we encourage you to explore our Azure capabilities and reach out with any questions.

Data Modernization in a Day is a hands-on workshop for TDMs, DBAs and other data roles that focuses on the value and process of migrating apps and databases to Azure.

Presented by Redapt data experts, this workshop focuses on helping customers who have moved infrastructure but no applications or data to Azure. Data Modernization in a Day focuses on the value and process of migrating apps and databases to Azure utilizing SQL Database, SQL Infrastructure as-a-Service (IaaS) and other data modernization Azure services and techniques.
