We're Proud to Present: The New Redapt

Written by Redapt Marketing | Jul 15, 2019 4:00:01 PM

We’re very excited to introduce you to the new, reimagined Redapt. It’s the culmination of over a year of work, the acquisition of Attunix, and much more.

Why rebrand Redapt?

Technology evolves at an incredible pace. To continue to provide you the best strategy and execution, we had to evolve as well.

Our new brand builds on our established reputation for providing clarity without complication — a straightforward, collaborative approach to helping you understand which technologies can best support your particular needs and goals.

Here’s the bottom line: As technology becomes even more complex, you can rely on us. We’ll help you navigate through any challenges and obstacles to accelerate growth.

Why does this matter to you?

In the face of constant change, your business must learn to leverage emerging technologies to maintain your competitive advantage. There is no third option.

Our new brand builds on our expertise, experience, and knowledge to expand the range of services and support we offer.

  • End-to-end consulting to help you find and leverage the right tech, whether you want to modernize your business or scale up your production
  • Infrastructure and systems integration that is second to none and trusted by the world’s biggest brands
  • Expertise across multiple clouds, which allows us to offer an array of cloud solutions without adhering to any single platform
  • Global reach as we expand into multiple offices across the country and increase support for our clients worldwide

[volcanno_heading title="What you can expect from us:"]Besides the exciting range of new services, expanded capabilities, and wider pool of talent, you can expect us to deliver the same high standard of service and customer support you’ve come to rely on. No matter what your technology needs are, from metal to cloud, we guarantee that:

  • Our team will provide clarity in a noisy and hard-to-navigate marketplace
  • We’re focused on building strong customer relationships and getting to know your business
  • We’re dedicated to serving you, not just selling solutions

Learn more about the new Redapt. Then let us help your business grow. We’re looking forward to it.
