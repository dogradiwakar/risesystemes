Datera Partners with Redapt

Written by Redapt Marketing | Jun 15, 2017 12:00:00 AM

[post_published]Supports Customer Demand for More Agile Data Management Solutions

Sunnyvale, California June 20, 2017 Datera Inc., a provider of elastic storage for on-premises clouds, today announced a strategic partnership with Redapt, a global systems integrator specializing in data center infrastructure and cloud engineering services. This partnership allows Redapt to distribute Datera's Elastic Data Fabric (EDF) software through all of its North American channels.

Datera EDF software offers enterprise-class performance coupled with cloud operations and agility for large organizations and service providers looking for scalable, on-premises clouds. This new partnership enables Redapt to provide its customer base with a complete, software-defined storage solution that runs on commodity x86 servers to deliver operational simplicity, economics and choice.

The datacenter is evolving at such a rapid rate that today's customers are seeking out new, transformative approaches to data storage," said Flavio Santoni, chief revenue officer of Datera. "We are demonstrating the ability to deliver game changing operational efficiency, scalability, infrastructure flexibility, and consistent high performance at dramatically lower TCO.  We are pleased to partner with Redapt to offer customers the agile data management solutions they need to best align to their business goals and succeed in their markets.

 

We frequently hear from customers that traditional storage models are rigid, expensive and resource constraining," said Matt Francis, senior director of storage engineering at Redapt. "They often ask us for better, more cost-effective solutions to data storage. Our new partnership with Datera will accelerate the delivery of true scale-out, software-defined storage solution for our customers, effectively lowering their storage TCO and increasing overall profitability.

 

About Datera

Datera is taking storage into the cloud era. Unlike traditional storage architectures that simply bridge to the public cloud, Datera Elastic Data Fabric (EDF) delivers a holistic blueprint to lead the architectural transformation to hybrid cloud data centers that enables unmatched simplicity, scalability and total cost of ownership, all at enterprise-class performance. Architected from the ground up to be operated as-a-service and run on multi-media x86 servers, Datera EDF eliminates storage management operations through policy driven automation and enables composable storage solutions with an unparalleled price/performance continuum in a no lock-in hardware model. Datera EDF operates at multi-rack scale across any orchestration stack, including VMware, Kubernetes, OpenStack, CloudStack, Docker and Mesosphere.
