New Release: COVID-19 Power BI Report Template

Written by Amie Mason | Apr 20, 2020 7:02:53 PM

As we continue to work closely with our clients to provide the necessary support for their digital transformation during this pandemic, we realize the need for up-to-date, localized, and easy-to-consume statistics tracking the progression of COVID-19.

Many organizations are relying on this data to make business-critical decisions, and government organizations are tasked with ensuring this data is readily available for consumption by their constituents.  

Customizable Power BI report template 

As part of a suite of newly developed and publicly available templates related to the COVID-19 crisis, Microsoft released a customizable Power BI report template based on data from USAFacts tracking daily and cumulative confirmed cases and total deaths attributed to the coronavirus in the U.S. at the state and county level. 

You can also use the underlying dataset to mash-up with other data to draw additional insights relevant to your needs.

 

 

           *Report best viewed on desktop.

Develop your version

We have made our version of the report available for public reference above. Still, we also want to enable other organizations and our SLG partners to develop their versions of the report. You can find documentation on the available template here.
