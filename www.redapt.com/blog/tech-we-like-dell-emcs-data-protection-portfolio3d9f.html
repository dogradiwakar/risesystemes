Tech We Like: Dell EMC’s Data Protection Portfolio

Written by Ross Peterson | Jul 21, 2020 3:00:00 PM

The recent rise in remote work has cast a renewed focus on the critical need for data protection.

With worker machines far from the watchful eyes of IT, ensuring your data is protected is absolutely critical—not just from potential bad actors, but to ensure your resilience in the face of potential disasters.

Failures in protection can be catastrophic for enterprises. Beyond substantial fines and, in some cases, Congressional hearings, allowing customer information to be exposed creates a distrust that stains a brand for years.

Internally, data breaches or loss of data can grind productivity to a halt, resulting in a substantial loss of revenue.

In these days where the goal for enterprises is to democratize data, you need solutions in place to make data protection easier.

In this edition of Tech We Like, we’re focusing on some of the powerful tools available in the Dell Data Protection Portfolio.

Dell EMC Integrated Data Protection Appliance (IDPA)

Also known as the DP4400, this integrated stack makes it very easy to roll out data production while also integrating with cloud recovery capabilities.

Included are complete backup, replication, recovery, instant access, and VMware integration. IDPA can also easily be scaled from 8TB to 96TB and delivers a deduplication rate that can reach 55:1.

Dell PowerProtect appliances

For managing data protection from a cloud—or multi-cloud—perspective, Dell PowerProtect hardware allows you to spin up the device in the cloud or on premises in order to consolidate your backups and recovery from anywhere.

PowerProtect comes in different flavors, including the X400 All Flash for demanding workloads and the X400 Hybrid, and are solid choices for helping you meet constantly changing governance requirements. 

Dell EMC PowerProtect Data Manager

To effectively monitor your data, you need a centralized visibility.

Dell EMC PowerProtect Data Manager is a software-defined solution that helps you manage data protection with a single interface, maintain self-service IT governance on premises and in the cloud, and meet evolving governance needs.
