IoT & Edge Solutions

IoT and edge solutions allow your organization to connect, process, store, and analyze device and sensor data with emerging technologies and best practices.

We can help you architect and deploy solutions that deliver deep insights for your organization.

The Redapt approach

We believe in helping our clients get the most out of IoT and Edge Solutions. We can assist you with:

  • Data & Analytics Strategy
  • Edge Computing & Storage
  • Data Platform Implementation
  • Reporting & Visualization
  • Machine Learning & Data Science

Core services

Redapt's advanced data and analytics solutions enable your organization to easily collect, analyze, and learn from data.

  • End-to-end analytics planning

    so your organization has a blueprint for how to best utilize data.

  • Edge design and deployment

    to help you select and implement the right edge and reduce data transport.

  • Bandwidth and latency optimization

    to run your workloads on the edge and reduce data transport.

  • Machine learning and data science

    to help your organization continually process and utilize data.
