From Data to Deployment

1The Data Layer
We Understand Data . Making sense of Data from various Business systems such as ERP, CRM , Support systems , ad hoc data and get a sense of business rules on top of those. This layer involves working with Business users to understand the real pain points and the analytical needs of the organization.
2The Integration & MDM Layer
Using ETL tools to collect , clean and compile data . Apply business rules to those and create batch / real time integrations to create warehouse / data models and keep incrementally updating the data . Populating MDM to create a single source of data which can be then used to populate various business systems .
3BI and Real Time Data Analytics
Connect all the Business Sytems and integrate data across multiple systems in real time and ad hoc mode to do real data analytics at scale. Use Enterprise Reporting tools / Custom tools to cater Business reporting needs .
